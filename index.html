Presentation structure

Consider structuring your 7-minute talk into clear sections:

  • 1–2 minutes: Who you are and your technical background.
  • 2–3 minutes: Problem, solution, and architecture of your capstone.
  • 2 minutes: Live or recorded demo of key features.
  • ~1 minute: Future learning and career direction.

Career materials & online presence

AI/ML targeting

ATS-optimized resume

  • Tailor your resume to AI/ML roles you are targeting.
  • Include keywords from job descriptions (tools, skills, methods).
  • Highlight bootcamp projects as “Experience” with measurable outcomes.

LinkedIn profile

  • Ensure your headline reflects your AI/ML or technical focus.
  • Add your bootcamp projects under “Projects” with links to GitHub and portfolio.
  • Use the same profile photo and branding as your website.

Professional online presence

  • GitHub profile with pinned repositories for all key projects.
  • Consistent repo structure, README files, and documentation.
  • Links between portfolio → GitHub → LinkedIn to create a clear loop.

Personal branding statement

Create a 1–2 sentence statement that highlights your unique skills, experience, and the value you bring to AI/ML-focused teams.

Example: “I am an emerging AI/ML practitioner with a strong foundation in data analysis and full-stack development, focused on building practical, production-ready solutions to real business problems.”
